# Phase 4: Expansion Checklist

**Before opening Location #2, make sure Location #1 is truly ready to be replicated.**

---

## Pre-Expansion Readiness Assessment

### Location #1 Must Be:

#### Financially Stable
- [ ] 2+ years of profitable operations
- [ ] Consistent 60%+ profit margins
- [ ] Predictable monthly revenue
- [ ] Cash reserves to cover 6+ months of expenses

#### Operationally Sound
- [ ] Systems documented and repeatable
- [ ] Team in place that can run without you daily
- [ ] Quality consistent across all services
- [ ] Client satisfaction high (NPS 8+)

#### Systematized
- [ ] Operations manual complete
- [ ] All processes documented
- [ ] Training materials ready
- [ ] Marketing playbook proven

#### Scalable
- [ ] You can step away for 2-4 weeks without crisis
- [ ] Team can handle day-to-day operations
- [ ] Growth is limited by capacity, not demand

---

## Expansion Models

### Model 1: Company-Owned Locations
You own and operate each facility.

**Best For:** Markets close to Location #1 (30-60 minutes)

### Model 2: Franchise / Licensing
Others operate facilities under your brand.

**Best For:** Markets far from Location #1 (2+ hours)

### Model 3: Joint Venture / Partnership
Partner with local operators.

**Best For:** Markets where you have strong local partner

---

## Target Markets

### Priority 1: Round Rock / Pflugerville
- Growing population, strong youth sports
- 30-45 minutes from Location #1

### Priority 2: San Antonio
- Large market (1.5M+ population)
- 45-60 minutes from Location #1

### Priority 3: Dallas
- Massive market, elite sports culture
- Only pursue if Locations #1-2 thriving

---

## Expansion Timeline (Per Location)

### Months 1-3: Market Testing
- [ ] Test demand with mobile services
- [ ] Secure 5-10 paying clients
- [ ] Build relationships with coaches/directors

### Months 4-6: Anchor Partner Acquisition
- [ ] Secure 1-2 anchor partners
- [ ] Pre-sell $20K-$30K in revenue
- [ ] Finalize location strategy

### Months 7-9: Site Selection & Funding
- [ ] Scout and negotiate lease
- [ ] Secure funding ($250K+)
- [ ] Finalize business structure

### Months 10-15: Build-Out
- [ ] Design, permitting, construction
- [ ] Hire and train local team

### Months 16-18: Launch & Ramp-Up
- [ ] Soft launch and grand opening
- [ ] Ramp to break-even within 6-12 months

---

## Success Metrics (Location #2, Year 1)

- [ ] Revenue: $250K+ (break-even minimum)
- [ ] 10-15 active memberships
- [ ] 5-10 team contracts
- [ ] You spending <50% of time there by Month 12

---

## When to Open Location #3

**Only when:**
- Location #1 thriving (3+ years, runs without you)
- Location #2 stable (1+ year, profitable)
- You have proven systems and capital
- You're not burned out

**Realistic Timeline:**
- Location #1: Year 1-3
- Location #2: Year 4-5
- Location #3: Year 6-7

**Don't rush. Build right, not fast.**
