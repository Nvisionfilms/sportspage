## Notes for Sales

### Positioning
- **Never lead with price** – Lead with value and results
- **Anchor high** – Show premium packages first, then scale down
- **Bundle aggressively** – Packages always better value than a la carte
- **Create urgency** – Limited spots, seasonal deadlines, early bird discounts

### Common Objections & Responses
**"That's expensive"**
→ "I totally understand. What were you expecting to invest? Let me show you what you get for that price..."

**"We'll just use our phone"**
→ "That works for some families! The difference is professional editing, recruiting-quality footage, and content kids actually want to post. Let me send you samples so you can see the difference."

**"Let me think about it"**
→ "Absolutely. What questions can I answer to help you decide? Also, we have [X] spots left for this season, so I don't want you to miss out."

### Upsell Opportunities
- Single game → 3-game package
- 3-game package → Seasonal package
- Seasonal package → Multi-sport or multi-year
- Team media day → Full season coverage
- Individual athlete → Bring teammates (team discount)
