# Pre-Launch Marketing Campaign

**Timeline:** 90 days before grand opening

---

## Campaign Objectives

1. **Build Awareness** – Make sure your target market knows the facility is coming
2. **Generate Pre-Sales** – Lock in memberships, rentals, and contracts before opening
3. **Create Buzz** – Get people talking and excited
4. **Establish Authority** – Position NVision as THE sports media destination

---

## 90-Day Campaign Timeline

### Days 90-60: Teaser Phase

**Goal:** Build curiosity and anticipation

#### Week 1-2 (Days 90-75)
**Social Media:**
- [ ] Post construction progress photos/videos
- [ ] "Something big is coming to [City]..." teasers
- [ ] Behind-the-scenes of build-out
- [ ] Poll followers: "What would you want in a sports media facility?"

**Email:**
- [ ] Email existing clients: "We're building something special..."
- [ ] Tease features without revealing everything
- [ ] Ask for feedback and input

**In-Person:**
- [ ] Tell every client you work with
- [ ] Hand out "Coming Soon" flyers at games/events
- [ ] Talk to coaches and directors

#### Week 3-4 (Days 75-60)
**Social Media:**
- [ ] Reveal first major feature (e.g., "We're building a full basketball court...")
- [ ] Time-lapse videos of construction
- [ ] "Guess what this will be?" posts
- [ ] Share vision and mission

**Content:**
- [ ] Blog post or video: "Why We're Building This"
- [ ] Share your story and vision
- [ ] Explain the problem you're solving

**Partnerships:**
- [ ] Reach out to schools/clubs about partnerships
- [ ] "We're opening soon—let's talk about how we can work together"

---

### Days 60-30: Reveal Phase

**Goal:** Show what you're building and start pre-sales

#### Week 5-6 (Days 60-45)
**Big Reveal:**
- [ ] Full facility reveal on social media
- [ ] Video walkthrough (even if not finished)
- [ ] Explain all features:
  - Basketball court
  - Turf system
  - Locker room set
  - Lighting and equipment
- [ ] "This is what we're building for YOU"

**Website/Landing Page:**
- [ ] Create facility landing page
- [ ] Features and benefits
- [ ] Pricing for memberships and rentals
- [ ] Pre-sale sign-up form
- [ ] FAQ section

**Email Campaign:**
- [ ] Email 1: "Here's what we're building"
- [ ] Email 2: "Why this matters for your athlete"
- [ ] Email 3: "Be one of the first—pre-sale opens [date]"

#### Week 7-8 (Days 45-30)
**Pre-Sale Launch:**
- [ ] Open pre-sales for:
  - Creator memberships
  - Facility rentals
  - Team contracts
  - Family packages
- [ ] Early bird discount (20-30% off)
- [ ] Limited spots (create urgency)

**Social Media Blitz:**
- [ ] Daily posts about features
- [ ] Testimonials from existing clients
- [ ] "Here's what you can do at the facility" content
- [ ] Countdown to grand opening

**Outreach:**
- [ ] Personal calls/texts to warm leads
- [ ] "We're opening in 30 days—want to be one of the first?"
- [ ] Offer exclusive tours for VIPs (coaches, directors, influencers)

---

### Days 30-7: Hype Phase

**Goal:** Maximize pre-sales and build excitement for opening

#### Week 9-10 (Days 30-15)
**Content Blitz:**
- [ ] Daily social media posts
- [ ] Behind-the-scenes of final setup
- [ ] Staff introductions (if hired)
- [ ] "Meet the team" content
- [ ] Equipment showcases

**Partnerships:**
- [ ] Announce partnerships with schools/clubs
- [ ] "We're proud to partner with [School/Club]"
- [ ] Co-marketing with partners

**Media Outreach:**
- [ ] Press release to local media
- [ ] Pitch story to local news stations
- [ ] Reach out to local sports bloggers/influencers
- [ ] Invite media to grand opening

#### Week 11-12 (Days 15-7)
**Final Push:**
- [ ] "Last chance for early bird pricing"
- [ ] Daily countdown posts
- [ ] Share pre-sale success ("X memberships sold!")
- [ ] Tease grand opening event
- [ ] Invite list for grand opening

**Email Campaign:**
- [ ] Email 1: "Grand opening in 2 weeks"
- [ ] Email 2: "Last chance for early bird pricing"
- [ ] Email 3: "You're invited to the grand opening"

---

### Days 7-0: Launch Week

**Goal:** Pack the grand opening and maximize day-one impact

#### Week 13 (Days 7-1)
**Daily Countdown:**
- [ ] Daily social media countdown
- [ ] Feature a different aspect of facility each day
- [ ] Share client testimonials
- [ ] Hype the grand opening event

**Grand Opening Event Planning:**
- [ ] Finalize event details:
  - Date and time
  - Activities (demos, tours, games)
  - Food/drinks
  - Giveaways
  - Special offers
- [ ] Invite list confirmed
- [ ] Media confirmed
- [ ] Photographer/videographer booked

**Final Outreach:**
- [ ] Personal invites to VIPs
- [ ] Reminder emails to pre-sale customers
- [ ] Final social media push

#### Day 0: Grand Opening
**Event Execution:**
- [ ] Doors open
- [ ] Welcome guests
- [ ] Facility tours
- [ ] Live demos (athletes using the space)
- [ ] Photo ops
- [ ] Sign-ups and bookings
- [ ] Capture EVERYTHING for content

**Social Media:**
- [ ] Live coverage on Instagram/Facebook
- [ ] Stories throughout the day
- [ ] Post highlights in real-time
- [ ] Encourage guests to post and tag

## Pre-Sale Offers

### Early Bird Memberships
- **Creator Membership (Basic):** $400/month (reg. $500) – First 10 members
- **Creator Membership (Pro):** $800/month (reg. $1,000) – First 5 members
- **Lock in pricing for 12 months**

### Facility Rentals
- **Pre-book 10 hours:** $2,000 (reg. $2,500)
- **Pre-book 20 hours:** $3,500 (reg. $5,000)
- **Use within 6 months of opening**

### Team Contracts
- **First 5 teams to sign:** 25% off first season
- **Multi-year contracts:** Additional 10% off

## Marketing Channels

### Social Media (Primary)
- **Instagram:** Daily posts, stories, reels
- **Facebook:** Events, community engagement
- **TikTok:** Behind-the-scenes, hype content
- **YouTube:** Long-form content, facility tours

### Email (Secondary)
- **Existing client list:** Your warmest audience
- **New subscriber list:** From landing page sign-ups
- **Frequency:** 2-3 emails per week during campaign

### In-Person (Critical)
- **At every game/event you cover:** Tell people
- **Hand out flyers:** "Coming Soon" cards with QR code
- **Word of mouth:** Your best marketing tool

### Partnerships (Leverage)
- **Schools/Clubs:** Ask them to share with their communities
- **Coaches/Directors:** Have them promote to their teams
- **Local Businesses:** Cross-promotion opportunities

### Paid Ads (Optional)
- **Facebook/Instagram Ads:** Target parents in your area
- **Google Ads:** Target searches like "sports photography [city]"
- **Budget:** $500-$1,000 for 90-day campaign

## Messaging Framework

### Core Message:
"The first sports media warehouse in Central Texas—where athletes become stars."

### Key Benefits:
- **For Athletes:** Professional content that gets you noticed
- **For Families:** Memories that last forever
- **For Teams:** Elevate your program's brand
- **For Creators:** Access to pro-level equipment and space

### Proof Points:
- **Experience:** "We've worked with [X] teams and [Y] athletes"
- **Quality:** "Over $20K in professional equipment"
- **Results:** "Our athletes have been recruited to [colleges]"
- **Community:** "Built by Central Texas, for Central Texas"

---

## Success Metrics

### Pre-Launch Goals (90 Days)
- [ ] 10-15 creator memberships pre-sold
- [ ] 20-30 facility rental hours pre-booked
- [ ] 5-10 team contracts signed
- [ ] 500+ email subscribers
- [ ] 2,000+ social media followers
- [ ] 100+ people at grand opening event

### Revenue Goal
- **Pre-Sales Target:** $30,000-$50,000 before opening day

---

## Contingency Plans

### If Pre-Sales Are Slow:
- Increase early bird discount
- Extend pre-sale period
- Offer more aggressive referral incentives
- Do more in-person outreach
- Host pre-opening tours for small groups

### If Construction Delays:
- Communicate transparently
- Keep audience engaged with updates
- Extend pre-sale offers
- Don't lose momentum—keep posting

---

**The pre-launch campaign is your chance to build demand BEFORE you open. Do this right, and you'll have customers waiting at the door on day one.**
